# Civilization flag assets

The PNG civilization flags in `civilization_flags/` are sourced from the
[AOE4World Explorer repository](https://github.com/aoe4world/explorer/tree/f5b5476188bc3d5fe279bd736aad31c98b8ede4d/assets/flags).
They are packaged so the desktop tracker remains usable without downloading
images at runtime.
